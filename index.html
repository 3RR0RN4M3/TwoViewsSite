Concept and Goals

Two Views is a 2-player local co-op puzzle solving game which is focused on visualizing the cause and the solution for reducing generation gap. It communicates the message via the story between two scientists, and the gameplay of shifting the view and their special abelites for solving the puzzles. We hope this game will increase the communication and relationship between children and parents.

Story Background

Two scientists are making a device to change the world, however the scientist thinks that their own ideology is the best. Both tried to implement their idea into the machine and a space anomaly occurs. Now they are trapped in a pocket dimension of their own and now they have to find a way to escape.

Gameplay

The core gameplay will be revolved around the 2 players exploring different versions of the same environment. Players often are required to adjust their views accordingly in order to solve some puzzles. The players will also have different ability for solving specific puzzles.
